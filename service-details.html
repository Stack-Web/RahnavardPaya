Service Details & About Us

Rahnavard Paya Co. introduced as RACO, was founded in 1995 as an Iranian international transport company with a focus on dedicated services. Transit via Iran to C.I.S countries and trucking service were the company’s major activities in the beginning. However, RACO expanded the size soon and became a reliable transport and logistics provider. Today Raco is an industry leader with professional range of service as air, sea, and road. Whether it's freight by road, air, or sea, RACO offers a complete and immediate response with an exceptional and personal service. In 2020, the company re-structured and reformed with the aim of taking over new challenges in the market. Customer orientation has been the main business approach of RACO over the years. We strive to continuously innovate and grow meeting the demands of the global marketplace. Catering to the individual needs of each client is something that sets us apart in competition. With us, it's all about focusing on customers’ satisfaction. Today we are proud of managing tones of trust globally.
